Projects

Scanotics :

It is a website that in the near future can be made into an app. This website will help individuals to examine their medical reports consisting of MRI Scans, X-Ray Scans and many more by a system that has been designed specifically to analyze these reports and send out the results to the individuals. And in case of any emergencies, this website also appoints a diagnosis with the doctor as soon as possible . And also the individuals have the freedom to opt for an online or an offline mode of consultation with the doctors. This was a group project created by the team KRAPT.

Technologies Used :

Javascript,Tenserflow,HTML/CSS,Python,Numpy,Jupyter

Tic Tac Toe :

This is a website for all age groups. I have created this project considering the growing interest of individuals in online games.
